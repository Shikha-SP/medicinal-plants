About Us

A student project for plant awareness

Nepal Medicinal Plant Detector is a student-made project created to help people notice and learn about the plants around them. Many useful and medicinal species grow in gardens, parks, and neighborhoods every day, yet they often go unrecognized.

We built this tool to make plant identification more accessible. By uploading a photo, anyone can explore what a plant might be and discover basic medicinal context, encouraging curiosity about local flora and a deeper connection with the natural world.

  • Student-led learning project
  • Designed to raise awareness of nearby plants
  • Focused on medicinal and useful species
